## CRITICAL: Broken Knowledge Pipeline — Full Audit

Here's every broken connection. I'm not going to sugarcoat any of it.

**What actually happens:**

| Data source | Written to | Read back by | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| Eval sidecar findings (113 JSONs) | `logs/evals/*.json` | `read_latest_eval_feedback()` → prompt_builder | **Only latest 1 file.** 112 files dead. |
| `write_learning()` — project-local | `.specs/learnings/general.md` | `_read_recent_learnings()` → codebase summary | **Capped at 40 lines.** Hundreds written, 40 read. |
| `write_learning()` — repo-level | `learnings/pending.md` | **NOTHING** | **Dead end.** 128 entries abandoned. |
| Repo L-series entries | `learnings/core.md`, `failure-patterns.md`, etc. | **NOTHING** | **Never injected into any build.** |
| MistakeTracker | In-memory only | prompt_builder (same campaign) | **Resets every campaign launch.** Cross-campaign = zero. |
| Auto-QA findings | Pipeline logs | **NOTHING** | **Never flows back.** |

**Five broken connections to fix:**

1. **`pending.md` is write-only** — 128 entries written by `write_learning()`, nothing reads them. Dead pipe.
2. **40-line learnings cap** — `_read_recent_learnings()` truncates at 40 lines. The sitdeck campaign wrote hundreds of lines of findings. Most are discarded.
3. **Repo-level learnings never injected** — 230+ L-series entries across 5 learnings files. None reach any agent. The entire knowledge base you've been building is invisible to the build loop.
4. **MistakeTracker is volatile** — it's a Python object that dies when the process exits. Next campaign starts fresh.
5. **No auto-promotion** — eval findings and pending entries never become L-series. The knowledge graph write path is the real fix, but until then, nothing bridges the gap.
